qua Cocktail topped with a miniature Floe finish

A refereshing tall glass of chilled water with fresh ice

Prep time: 5 mins / Cooking: 3-4 hours / Portions 1

Ingredients:

  • 2 cup Water
  • 1 Ice Tray
  • 1 glass
  • Optional: A wedge of lemon

Preparation:

  1. Precool freezer to 0°F(-180°C).

  2. Prepare 1 cup of water in ice tray.

  3. Once freezer is cooled down to the right temperature, place ice tray inside and let it sit for 3-4 hours.

  4. Prepare another cup of water into a cup or glass, and set aside for later.

  5. After 3-4 hours, take out freshly frozen water and carefully add to the glass of regular water.

  6. Serve water with a wedge of lemon for added taste.
